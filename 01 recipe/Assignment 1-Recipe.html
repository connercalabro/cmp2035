Salmon with Spring Vegetables

Ingredients

  • 4 (6-ounce) boneless, skinless salmon fillets
  • 4 (6-ounce) boneless, skinless salmon fillets
  • Kosher Salt
  • Freshly Ground Pepper
  • 2 Large eggs
  • 1 small shallot, minced
  • 1 small garlic clove, minced
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 8 ounces asparagus
  • 1 cup shelled English peas
  • 1/2 cup coarsely chopped and loosely packed herbs

Cooking/Preparation Steps

  1. Line a baking sheet with lightly greased parchment paper
  2. Season salmon with kosher salt and pepper
  3. Bake at 325Â° for 12 to 15 minutes or until salmon is no longer translucent but still moist-looking in center
  4. Place eggs in small saucepan, and cover with water
  5. Bring to a boil; remove from heat, cover, and let stand 15 minutes
  6. Rinse eggs with cold water, and let cool--Peel, dice, and set aside
  7. Whisk together shallot and next 3 ingredients in a medium bowl and season with kosher salt and pepper
  8. Bring a 2-quart saucepan of salted water to a boil
  9. Cook asparagus 30 to 60 seconds or until crisp-tender; lift out with a slotted spoon, rinse with cold water, and add to vinaigrette
  10. Cook peas in same boiling water until just cooked, about 1 minute
  11. Drain in a colander, rinse with cold water, and add to asparagus and vinaigrette and sprinkle in herbs, and toss gently
  12. Transfer salmon to plates using a spatula
  13. Spoon asparagus mixture evenly over salmon and sprinkle diced eggs over vegetables
