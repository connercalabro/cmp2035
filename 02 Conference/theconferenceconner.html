Investigating Female Professional Sports Conference 2017


Details of the Event

  • What:

    First annual conference to discuss components of Female Professional Sports including the personal insight of pro female athletes, the marketing/publicity behind female individuals and teams, finances and pay, and the juxtaposition versus their male counterpoarts

  • When:

    June 2-4, 2017

  • Where:

    Tufts University
